SANIATA FESTIVAL

The Saniata Festival is the municipal festival of Burgos, Ilocos Norte, which celebrates local folklore and its famous dragon fruit cultivation. It highlights the town's journey from its traditional roots to becoming a thriving community known for producing dragon fruit. The festival showcases the vibrant spirit of the town and its people.
