DISCOVER THE BEAUTY OF BURGOS

Burgos is a peaceful coastal town in Ilocos Norte known for its wind turbines, dramatic rock formations, and Spanish-era lighthouse. Visitors can enjoy the fresh ocean breeze and breathtaking views of the West Philippine Sea.
